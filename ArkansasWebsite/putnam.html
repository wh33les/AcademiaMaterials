UArk William Lowell Putnam Page

The William Lowell Putnam Mathematics Competition is one of the most prestigious math competitions in the US. Here are the 2016 fliers.

The test consists of a 3 hour morning session with 6 problems, followed by a 2 hour lunch break, followed by a 3 hour afternoon session with 6 different problems. Each problem is worth 10 points for a total of 120 possible.

If you would like to participate, contact Dr. Wheeler ashleykw (at) uark (dot) edu before 25 November, 2016. Right now there is a waiting list.
